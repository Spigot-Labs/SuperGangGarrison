OpenGarrison Bot Work Failure Handoff

This document intentionally replaces the prior MLBot handoff content. The ML training direction and the later deterministic-oracle pivot did not produce a releasable bot fix.

What failed

- Broad ML/IL/RL work did not produce reliable scoring across TwodFortTwo. The bots remained brittle around traversal, especially the alternating stair/final-intel approach.
- Unified/class-general training regressed previously working cases. Blue Scout and other classes still got caught or failed to complete full score chains.
- Extra V5/V6 data, DAgger/intervention clips, staged chunks, reward tweaks, and traversal affordance ideas did not create a proven headless self-improving loop.
- The local movement oracle search was too slow and incomplete. It could solve some isolated pockets but failed the mirrored/right-side TwodFortTwo staircase case.
- The mirror fallback was invalid. The opposite-side tape replayed to the right x position but stayed on the lower platform, so it was rejected.
- A runtime bridge-edge attempt made Blue Scout worse: after adding bridge edges from lower-right nodes into upper stair nodes, Blue Scout failed before pickup.
- No model or deterministic bot patch from this effort should be promoted.

Concrete failed evidence

- Red Pyro TwodFortTwo Score did pass once, but that did not generalize.
- Blue Pyro TwodFortTwo Score passed once after direct route-miss fallback, but the path remained fragile and not a general fix.
- Blue Scout TwodFortTwo Score failed after pickup before the last pass, with return progress stuck around the home staircase.
- Blue Scout local movement oracle right-side climb failed:

Recommended next direction

- Do not continue broad ML model promotion.
- Do not rely on the failed local movement oracle as implemented.
- Treat TwodFortTwo bot release readiness as unresolved.
- The next engineer should start from source/runtime proof, not from these failed assumptions.
- If a release must ship soon, use narrow deterministic route/behavior fixes with end-to-end score proof per class/team/map, or disable unsupported bot claims for release.

Current acceptance bar

- Do not claim success from graph reachability or local route progress.
- A valid fix must pass full scenario scoring, not just pickup or arrival.
- Minimum proof for TwodFortTwo should include full `Score` for both teams and held-out classes, with no hidden deterministic ML-policy overrides.
