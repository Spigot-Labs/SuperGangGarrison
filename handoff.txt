New minimal system shape:

Objective chooses a route.
It does not steer.

Route becomes a corridor program.
Flatten route links into ordered progress points plus capability annotations.

One corridor runner owns movement.
State is only:

current segment
current progress index/order
desired direction
current accepted contact
launch state: seeking launch, stable on launch, launched, relay/contact recovery
recovery budget
Links become metadata.
They only say “this span may need jump/drop/hold down,” not “cursor is link N and must match FromSurfaceId.”

Session cannot independently steer while inside corridor.
It can only:

keep corridor runner active
ask corridor runner for local recovery
abandon route if outside accepted corridor/recovery band
